HEALTH AND LIFE EXPECTANCY ACROSS THE GLOBE

Life Expectancy

Life expectancy is an important indicator of the overall health of a population. It is influenced by various factors such as access to healthcare, lifestyle, and environmental conditions.


Some countries have a life expectancy of over 80 years, while others have a life expectancy of less than 60 years. It is important to understand these differences and work towards improving the health and well-being of people across the globe.
